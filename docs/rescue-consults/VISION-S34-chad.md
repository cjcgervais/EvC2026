## Structured pillars
1. **Escalating squirrels** — rescued squirrels grow bigger over a run (visible progression).
2. **COORDINATED CATCH (the emotional core)** — the rescued squirrels riding your back CATCH the incoming
   newbs themselves: the little ones find the courage to jump to their friends on your back, and the
   bigger squirrels collectively snag them = a growing rescue crew. "The ones you saved help save more."
   Directly extends the BUILT living-rider (#2) + catch/climb (#9) systems.
3. **Drama + intense music** — stakes are felt; audio escalation.
4. **ENVIRONMENT-VARIED MISSIONS** — the environment changes HOW you fly:
   - Fire in the squirrel village → grab/carry a **water cannon** → one-shot the fire.
   - **Fox** on a hillside → escape/evade mission.
   - (framework for more) → each environment = a different flying mission.
5. **Story + HELPER-BIRD unlocks** — progress a narrative; unlock helper birds that fly with you
   (reuses the 4-bird SQUAD + possession + formation tech; ties to the earlier possum/raccoon-rescuer
   and RC-plane-squadron ideas in the bible / [[project-updraft-direction]]).
6. **STEALTH MATH (headline differentiator)** — a perfect **10/10 rescues in good time** earns a
   **time-factor bonus multiplier**; the multiply-metric quietly teaches kids math (ages 5-20).
